EBEC Final

The Final Round of EBEC is the event gathering the winning teams of all the National and Regional Rounds.

This year, it will happen on the 22-30th of July 2022, in Zagreb.

EBEC Iberia

A National and Regional Round of EBEC is the event gathering the winning teams of the Local Rounds.

This year, EBEC Iberia will happen on the 13-16th of May 2022, in Porto, gathering the winning teams from the Local Rounds of Coimbra, Madrid Carlos III, Aveiro, Lisbon, Madrid, Almada and Porto.

EBEC Coimbra

A Local Round of EBEC is an event organised by a local BEST group and it is the first phase of the European BEST Engineering Competition (EBEC).

EBEC Coimbra will happen on the 4, 5, 6 and 9 of March 2022 and it is characterised by being a 24h competition, completely free, and open to all the students of the Faculty of Sciences and Technology of the University of Coimbra.

Choose your category

Case Study

Case Study is a theoretical problem-solving challenge that requires analysing, researching, deliberating, testing and presenting a solution that fits a real-case scenario based on realistic resources within a limited time.

Innovative Design

Innovative Design is a practical challenge, where competitors should combine their creativity and knowledge to develop new technological designs or improve upon existing ones. The project must have a technical, social, environmental and economic impact.

Lecturer or phD student?

Teacher’s BEST Engineering Competition (TBEC) is an extension from EBEC Coimbra. It is open to all lecturers and phD students from the Faculty of Sciences and Technology of the University of Coimbra and it happens simultaneously with EBEC Coimbra.

Meet the organization

BEST (Board of European Students of Technology) is a non-profit, non-political and non-governmental organisation whose main objective is to increase diversity by developing science and technology students across Europe.

BEST Coimbra is the local BEST group of the University of Coimbra, active since 1992 at the Faculty of Sciences and Technology of the University of Coimbra (FCTUC). Throughout the academic year, it holds several events, such as engineering competitions, job fairs and workshops for FCTUC students, as well as BEST Courses, attended by students from all over Europe.
